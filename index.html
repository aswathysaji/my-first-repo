About Us

What is SHE?

Aims to guide the girls in our college with -Exposure -Mentorship -Experience required to face the career demands of a workforce that has only 38% women in leadership positions.

Short term objectives

    • -To promote diverse opportunities to develop technical capabilities and talents among female students.
      -To develop student leaders who will be the torch bearers of the second phase.
      -To initiate a women for empowerment in GCE Kannur.

    • Long term objectives

    • -To encourage women engineers to take up leadership roles.
      -To promote greater enrolment of girls in engineering.

    • Why SHE?

    • -Eventhough the participation of women in undergraduate levels is satisfactory,their representation in higher positions of workspace isn't.
      -Here's the NEED for the right mentorship and relatable role models.

    • Background of SHE

    171 Engineering college with an enrolment ratio of girls-36% GCE Kannur catering to the malabar region of kerala.51% of the annual intake of UG in GCEK are girls.

    Mode of Operation

    The coordinators of SHE program have well identified the value of our resourceful alumni and have ensured their fruitful participation in gathering suggestions and information.The other promising contributors are the teaching fraternity of GCEK,resource persons from external agencies and experts from different walks of life.The SHE program also seeks all the support from PTA and the HRD cell of GCEK.
